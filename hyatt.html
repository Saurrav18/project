PET POLICY:

Pets can weigh up to a maximum of 15 kilograms. One pet per room or suite is welcome. Additional charges of 5,000 INR, plus taxes per night per pet, is applicable. The hotel offers a bed, food, dry food and water bowl to pets during their stay. Pets are prohibited from entering the health club, spa, restaurants or back-of-the-house areas. Pets should not be left unattended at any point during their stay. The hotel will levy charges for any destruction or damage to hotel property. The hotel holds the right to refuse admission of any pet entering the premises. In light of any damage to hotel property, disruption or disturbance to other guests, any pet and owner be asked to leave the premises. In light of any physical harm caused to guests, hotel associates or themselves, the guest is responsible for medical charges. The hotel is not liable for the loss or accident of a pet on or off the premises. Pets must be fully vaccinated, neutered and chipped. All vaccinations must be up-to-date and a copy of the certificate must be presented on request.
